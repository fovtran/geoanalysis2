The Higgs Field

Higgs Bosons

  • Has non-trivial vacuum expectation
  • Spontaneously breaks SU(2) x U(1)Y gauge symmetry to the U(1)em gauge symmetry
  • In U(1)em Quarks, Leptons, and gauge fields gain mass through spontaneous symmetry breaking
  • Higgs Boson was discovered in 2012
